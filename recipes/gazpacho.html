Talya's Gazpacho

Description

The perfect summer dinner! A refreshing bowl of chilled soup can't be beat on a hot summer evening. It takes just about 10 minutes and is highly customizable. Toss in some cucumbers, some red onion, maybe even some watermelon! Whatever you have on hand can be thrown in for a fun new flavor.

If you've got a bit more time and want a bit of a heartier dish, scroll down for Marina's Croutons, which can be tossed on top for an extra crunch and a pop of flavor!

Ingredients

NOTE: anything can be adjusted to your taste! have some fun messing around with it.

  • 2 lbs tomato chunks (skin optional). Any size, any flavor, just make sure they're ripe!
  • 1 Anaheim pepper, cored, seeded to your taste, roughly chopped
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and chunked
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tsp sherry vinegar (add some ACV for a twist), more to taste
  • salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup evoo, plus a lil for drizzing

Steps

  1. Toss it all in a blender
  2. Adjust to taste
  3. Drizzle with olive oil to serve

et voila!

If you're into a bit of crunch and have a bit more time, read on!

Marina's Croutons

  1. Find a stale loaf/baguette
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  3. Tear/slice it into half inch cubes
  4. Toss it on a baking sheet with some olive oil (I like to use bacon grease if I'm feeling decadent)
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes
  6. Serve on top of gazpacho
