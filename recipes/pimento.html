Pili's Pimento Cheese

Description

The perfect spread for any sandwich, any cracker, and smorgasboard! I like to spread mine on some rye, toss some pickles and tomatoes on top and call it a day!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 jalapeno, seeded (to your taste) and minced
  • 4oz jar of diced pimentos, drained
  • salt and pepper to taste

Depending on my mood and what I have in the kitchen I might throw in some grated jack, some dill, or if I'm feeling fancy, some pickled red onion.

Steps

  1. Put it all in a big bowl
  2. Roll up your sleeves, stretch out your arms a bit
  3. Mix, mix, mix! Really get it all evenly mixed. Protip: I like to use a spatula. It works better than anything else I've tried

Delicious, huh? Thanks Pili!
